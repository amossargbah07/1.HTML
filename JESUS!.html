A few fact about our lord and personal saviour Jesus Christ

Jesus of Nazareth, was a 1st-century Jewish preacher and religious leader. He is the central figure of Christianity, the world's largest religion.Jesus is the incarnation of God the Son an awaited messiah, or Christ, a descendant from king David line that is prophesied in the Old Testament. Virtually all modern scholars of antiquity agree that Jesus existed historically.Accounts of Jesus's life are contained in the Gospels, especially the four canonical Gospels in the New Testament. Since the Enlightenment, academic research has yielded various views on the historical reliability of the Gospels and how closely they reflect the historical Jesus.

About Jesus Christ

  • Jesus was a real person who lived in first-century Judea.
